Ballpark Style Sloppy Joes

Prep: 10min
Cook: 30min
Calories: 890
Feeds: 2

Ballpark Style Sloppy Joes with Mozzarella Cheese, Green Pepper & Potato Wedges
Description:

Tired of the same ol' Sloppy Joes that ma' makes? Us too, these Ballpark Style Sloppy Joes are the real deal! We'll show you how to create an amazing ketchup and mustard spiked sauce that will bring back the best memories of your childhood.

Ingredients:
  • 12oz of Yukon Gold Potatoes
  • 1 Yellow Onion
  • 1 Green Pepper
  • 1 Clove of Garlic
  • 2TBSP of Ketchup
  • 2tsp of Dijon Mustard
  • 6ml Beef Stock Concentrate
  • 6ml Ponzu Sauce
  • 1TBSP Cornstarch
  • 10oz Ground Beef
  • 1/2 cup Mozzarella Cheese
  • 2 Potato Buns
Steps:
  1. Wash potatoes and cut into 1/2 inch wedges
  2. Toss potatoes on a baking sheet with salt and pepper and a drizzle of olive oil
  3. Roast on top rack for 20-25 mins
  4. Thinly slice the green pepper, half the onion and peel and mince garlic
  5. Combine ketchup, mustard, stock, ponzu, cornstarch and 1/3 cup of water in a small bowl
  6. Cook veggies over medium to high heat for 4-6 minutes
  7. Cook beef in medium to high heat for 4-6 minutes and add in garlic and sauce
  8. Drizzle mozzarella over the beef until it melts, toast the buns
  9. Place beef and veggies inside the buns and place on a plate with potato wedges and enjoy!
Turkey Meatball Penne

Prep: 5min
Cook: 20min
Calories: 870
Feeds: 2

Turkey Meatball Penne with Tomato Cream Sauce and Parmesan
Description:

It looks like it costs a pretty Penne! This amazing dish will make you feel like a champion. If you don't try this recipe, it will haunt you daily. With a cook time of only 20 minutes, we can assure you that you'll be satisfied and getting back to your busy life in no time!

Ingredients:
  • 1 Shallot
  • 8oz Pre Cooked Meatballs
  • 6oz of Penne Pasta
  • 1 Clove of Garlic
  • 4oz of Cream Sauce Base
  • 1TBSP of Italian Seasoning
  • 1/4 cup of Parmesan Cheese
  • 1tsp of Chili Flakes
  • 3oz of Tomato Paste
Steps:
  1. Boil a large pot of salted water, peel and mince shallot and garlic
  2. Brown the meatballs in a pan with a drizzle of olive oil for 3-5 mins
  3. Cook pasta in large pot for 9-12 mins
  4. Combine shallot, garlic, italian seasoning, chili flakes and tomato paste with 2TBSP of butter in a pan over medium heat
  5. Add cream sauce and parmesan to the pan for 2-3 mins and season with salt and pepper
  6. Stir in meatballs and penne into pan with sauce
  7. Top with a pinch of parmesan cheese and serve!
Beef & Cheese Tostadas

Prep: 10min
Cook: 40min
Calories: 960
Feeds: 2

Beef & Cheese Tostadas with green peppers, tomato salsa and hot sauce crema
Description:

These Tostadas are nothing like the food at your local Mexican restaurant. These are high quality, great tasting, round UFO's of awesomeness! We bet you can't eat just one, no really we bet you $25 dollars you can't eat just one! (Sign up to receive weekly recipes for a chance to win a $25 dollar giftcard, restrictions apply)

Ingredients:
  • 1 Roma Tomato
  • 1/4oz of Cilantro
  • 1 Yellow Onion
  • 1 Lime
  • 1 Green Pepper
  • 4TBSP of Sour Cream
  • 6ml Beef Stock Concentrate
  • 1tsp of Hot Sauce
  • 1TBSP of Southwest Spice Blend
  • 10oz Ground Beef
  • 1tsp of Chili Powder
  • 6 Flour Tortillas
  • 1/2cup of Mexican Cheese Blend
Steps:
  1. Chop tomato, cilantro, onion, lime, and green pepper
  2. Combine tomato, cilantro, sour cream, onion, juice from the lime, salt, pepper and hot sauce into a small bowl
  3. Cook beef in a pan for 4-5 mins with southwest spice chili powder and a pinch of salt
  4. Once the beef is browned, add in stock concentrate, green pepper, sliced onion, and a pinch of salt
  5. Bake tortillas with some olive oil on a baking sheet for 4-5 mins
  6. Load tortillas with beef, Mexican cheese, salsa crema and remaining lime juice and enjoy!
